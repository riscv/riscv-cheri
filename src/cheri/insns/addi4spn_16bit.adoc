==== {C_ADDI4SPN_CAP}  ({cheri_base_ext_name})

Synopsis::
{C_ADDI4SPN_CAP_DESC}

Mnemonic::
`{C_ADDI4SPN_CAP} {cd}', nzuimm`

Expansion::
`{caddi} {cd}', {abi_creg}sp, nzuimm`

Encoding::
include::wavedrom/c-ciw.adoc[]

Description::

Copy `{abi_creg}sp` to `{cd}` (`{abi_creg}sp={creg}2`). Add a zero-extended non-zero immediate, scaled by 4, to `{cd}'.address`.
+
All other details, including the conditions for clearing `{cd}'.tag`, are as defined by <<CADDI>> for the expansion shown above.

Exceptions::

+
Exceptions occur when the authorizing capability fails one of the checks listed below:
+

| {cheri_excep_name_ld}      | {cheri_excep_desc_perm} <<r_perm>> is required.
| {cheri_excep_name_ld}      | {cheri_excep_desc_bnds}
| {cheri_excep_name_ld}      | {cheri_excep_desc_intg}

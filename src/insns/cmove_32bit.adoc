==== CMV

include::new_encoding_note.adoc[]

ifdef::cheri_v9_annotations[]
NOTE: *CHERI v9 Note:* This instruction was called CMOVE.
endif::[]

Synopsis::
Capability move

Mnemonic::
`cmv cd, cs1`

Suggested assembly syntax::
`mv cd, cs1`

NOTE: all mnemonics must be unique, but the intention is to always use `mv` in the
 assembly syntax and distinguish `mv/cmv` by operand type.

Encoding::
include::wavedrom/cmv.adoc[]

NOTE: <<CMV>> is encoded as <<CADD>> with `rs2=x0`.

Description::
The contents of capability register `cs1`  are written to capability register
`cd`. <<CMV>> unconditionally moves the whole capability to `cd` .

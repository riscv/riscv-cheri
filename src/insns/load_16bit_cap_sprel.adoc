==== C.LCSP

Synopsis::
Capability loads (C.LC, C.LCSP), 16-bit encodings

pass:attributes,quotes[{cheri_cap_mode_name}] Mnemonics::
`c.lc cd', offset(cs1'/csp)`

pass:attributes,quotes[{cheri_cap_mode_name}] Expansions::
`lc cd', offset(cs1'/csp)`

Encoding::
include::wavedrom/c-sp-load-cap.adoc[]

pass:attributes,quotes[{cheri_int_mode_name}] Description::
These mnemonics do not exist in pass:attributes,quotes[{cheri_int_mode_name}]. The RV32 encodings map to <<C.FLW>>/<<C.FLWSP>> and the RV64 encodings map to <<C.FLD>>/<<C.FLDSP>>.
